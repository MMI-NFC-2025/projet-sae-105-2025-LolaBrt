Glossary

Here are some important terms to better understand the story of Margaret Hamilton and the world of science and technology:

  • NASA

    United States space agency, responsible for civilian space programs.

  • Apollo

    American space program aimed at sending men to the Moon.

  • Software

    Set of programs that make a computer work.

  • Bug

    Error in a computer program.

  • Algorithme

    Suite d'instructions pour résoudre un problème.

  • MIT

    Prestigieuse université américaine spécialisée en science et technologie.

  • Algorithme

    Suite d'instructions pour résoudre un problème.

  • Programming

    Activity of writing code to create software.

  • Software Engineering

    Discipline applying rigorous methods to software development.

  • Lunar Module (LEM)

    Spacecraft that landed on the Moon.

  • Matilda Effect

    Forgetting or minimizing the contributions of women scientists.

  • STEM

    Acronyme pour Sciences, Technologies, Ingénierie et Mathématiques.

  • Racial Segregation

    Forced separation of people based on their ethnic origin.

  • Gender Discrimination

    Unfair treatment of a person based on their gender.
