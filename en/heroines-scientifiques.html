Scientific Heroines

Here are gathered films, books and stories, real or fictional, that talk about women scientists. Often sidelined in their time, they are now recognized for their courage, their intelligence and their determination. These works remind us that they played an essential role in scientific discoveries and that it is important to give them the recognition they deserve.
